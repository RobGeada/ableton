This impulses are free, all are based on the name of the cab and using a neutral poweramp and API & Neve mic preamps
Use with an IR loader like Pulse by Rosen Digital, Recabinet, Torpedo wall of sound and many more.
If you like the IRs and you want to support me and tell you are enjoying the IRs, subscribe to my channel for motivate me to make more and more IR.


"Because a budget bass player or guitar player can sound great using free stuff".
Cabs fully compatible with Helix, Amplifire 12, Recabinet 5, TH3-THU , BIAS AMP1,BIAS AMP 2 Rosen Digital Pulse and many more

The letters on the single IR are:
A for a bright tone
B for a medium tone
C for a dark tone

the single mic mixes had the same idea with the letters A,B,C, here I use the same mic on diferent positions to blend them and get a better tone
